  • Payroll Services

    Payroll administration can be time consuming. As a business owner and an employer in the UK, you may have to operate PAYE (Pay As You Earn) as a part of your payroll. PAYE is a system that HM Revenue and Customs’ (HMRC) use to collect Income Tax and National Insurance Contributions from the employees’ earnings. You need to report and pay HMRC every month. Managing all these is not easy, especially for small businesses.
    The service we provide is flexible, processing your payroll from start to finish leaving you free to focus on running your business.
        Monthly / weekly / fortnightly payroll processing service
        Set up new PAYE reference numbers if required
        Payroll preparation: including payslips and payroll reports
        RTI submissions to HMRC: Real Time Information for PAYE
        Manage pension administration
        Handling all starters and leavers
        P60’s

    We can also assist you with Auto Enrolment using NEST :-

  • Making Tax Digital ( MTD )

    Making Tax Digital (MTD) came into effect in April 2019 affecting all VAT Registered businesses. It is a fundamental change to the UK tax system, affecting how UK businesses maintain their accounting records.

    All businesses with turnover above the VAT registration threshold will be required to maintain their accounting records DIGITALLY using a recognised accounting software. ( Recognised by the HMRC )

    Businesses which do not maintain digital accounting records or submit their VAT Return to HMRC digitally will face financial penalties.

    Software such as Xero, Quickbooks and Sage Business Cloud Accounts are MTD ready and use of these software will ensure that a business is MTD compliant.
